James Otto is a current graduate student studying Statistics at Baylor University. He holds undergraduate degrees in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, and Management of Information Systems. James takes great interest in the studies of computational statistics and decision theory, and he enjoys exploring the connections between theoretical mathematics and the field of statistics. When not working on his research, James can often be found going on hikes, rock climbing, or reading the works of J. R. R. Tolkien and Kurt Vonnegut.
